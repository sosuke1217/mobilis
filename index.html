Mobilisが大切にしている3つの動き

The 3 Core Movements

伸ばす

一般的なストレッチだけでは届きにくいインナーマッスルにアプローチします。凝り固まった身体の奥深くを緩めることで、本来の柔軟性と可動域を取り戻すことができます。

動かす

整った身体を実際に「動かす」ことで、新しい動きの感覚を身につけていきます。神経と筋肉のつながりを再建築することが、日常の動作を自然と軽やかに変えていきます。

定着させる

一回のセッションで終わらせず、お客様ご自身の気づきと実践を大切にしています。身体の変化を記録し、振り返ることで、良い状態を無意識にでも維持できるようサポートします。

Stretch

Releasing deep muscle tension helps the body soften, breathe more freely, and move with greater ease. Which unlocks a fuller range of motion from the inside out.

Move

Change begins not with passive stretching, but with conscious movement. When the body moves with intention, the nervous and muscular systems reconnect, allowing everyday actions to feel lighter and more fluid.

Integrate

Change takes more than a single session. By journaling, your progress becomes clearer. The more you notice, the more your body responds and with time, it becomes second nature.

Born in Taiwan with Japanese-Taiwanese heritage, I spent 4 years studying at the University of Hawaii and completed a year-long internship, developing myself in an international environment. Through diverse sports including soccer, swimming, tennis, and basketball, I learned the importance of understanding the body.

Based in Tokyo, I work as a stretch trainer supporting a wide range of clients from desk workers to busy parents and athletes seeking performance improvement. My approach is grounded in anatomy-based scientific methods, focusing on sustainable change and habit formation rather than temporary relaxation.

Q1普通のストレッチとどう違うの？

Mobilisは深部筋へのアプローチと習慣化を重視したパーソナルストレッチサービスです。 場合によっては運動要素を取り入れ、柔軟性だけでなく、動きやすさやパフォーマンス向上もサポートします。

Q2 どんな服装が良いですか？

こちらで準備は致しませんので、動きやすくリラックスできる服装（ジャージ・ヨガウェア等）をご用意ください。

Frequently Asked Questions

Q1 How is it different from regular stretching?

Mobilis offers personalized sessions focused on deep muscle release and habit formation. We also incorporate movement and exercise elements when appropriate, supporting not only flexibility but also improved mobility and performance.

Q2 What should I wear?

Please prepare a comfortable clothing that allows movement (e.g., tracksuits, yoga wear).

Q3 When will I see results?

Many clients feel improvements in range of motion or posture after just one session. Results vary by individual.

Q4 Is booking required?

Yes, sessions are by appointment only. Please book via LINE, Whatsapp, or our web contact form.

Q5 Will you bring a bed?

Yes, we bring a professional portable stretch table (approx. 70cm wide, 185cm long).
Please make sure there’s at least 2m × 1m of space available. If you prefer to use the floor, let us know in advance.

Q6 What if I don't have enough space at home?

If securing space at home is difficult, sessions can also be held at rental studios or shared spaces.
You can either arrange the venue yourself, or let us know your preferred area and we can suggest suitable options.
*Please note that any rental fees will be charged separately at cost.
